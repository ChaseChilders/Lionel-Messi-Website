Barcelona Arrival

Messi was only 16 years old when he first made his debut with Barcelona's first team. He went on as a super-sub in a friendly against Porto. His performance in this game allowed him to find a practice spot on the first team and eventually become a starting player. By the time he was 18 years old, he signed a contract with Barcelona with a buyout clause of over 150 million.

What made Messi the best?

  • His ability to create space and dribble out of tight situations
  • His pure speed and quickness
  • Natural shooting ability with both feet
  • His understanding of the game of soccer on a level deeper than his counterparts
